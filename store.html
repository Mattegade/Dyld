Giffords Hall
Suffolk

Giffords Hall is a family run vineyard located in the village of Hartest in Suffolk. We produce a range of English wines including superb East Anglian Sparkling wines, award winning rosé, aromatic Bacchus, a wonderfully fruity red Pinot Noir and many more!

Coates & Seely
Hampshire

Hidden in the heart of England’s chalk downlands, our vineyards produce distinctive wines that are above all an expression of the uniqueness of our chalk terroir. Small-scale, artisanal production allows us to make our wines to the most exacting standards of winemaking craftsmanship.

Chapel Down
Kent

Chapel Down is England's leading winemaker and one of the UK's most exciting drinks companies. We produce a world-class range of sparkling and still wines and beautiful gin and vodka & cider. Our sparkling wines are some of the best in World and all created from fruit grown in England.
